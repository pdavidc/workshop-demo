WMS and WMTS Layers

Globe imagery may be sourced from Open Geospatial Consortium (OGC) Web Map Service (WMS) or Web Map Tile Service (WMTS) and displayed on the globe. WebWorldWind provides functionality to query a service, review the available maps, and add them as visible layers to the globe. Image resolution and bounds are automatically determined and configured by WebWorldWind
